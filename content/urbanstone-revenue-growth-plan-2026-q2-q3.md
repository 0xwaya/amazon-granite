# Urban Stone Revenue Growth Plan (Q2-Q3 2026)

## 1) Revenue Targets
- Primary goal: increase monthly closed revenue by 25-35% by end of Q3 2026.
- Pipeline goal: 2.2x monthly qualified lead volume versus current baseline.
- Conversion goal: raise lead-to-appointment rate to 35%+ and appointment-to-close rate to 45%+.

## 2) Offer Ladder (Money-Making Core)
- Entry offer: free estimate + curated slab shortlist in 24h.
- Mid-ticket anchor: kitchen package with sink + install timeline guarantee window.
- Upsell lane: backsplash upgrades, premium edge profiles, cutout complexity upgrades.
- Contractor lane: repeat-unit pricing and priority scheduling for 3+ unit jobs.

## 3) Lead Capture System
- Website:
  - Keep chat-to-estimate handoff after intent signals (sqft + material + timeline).
  - Add sticky "Get Estimate in 24h" CTA above fold on key pages.
  - Add 2-step estimate flow for mobile (contact first, project details second).
- Speed-to-lead:
  - Auto-SMS/email acknowledgment in <1 minute.
  - First human follow-up in <10 minutes during business hours.
- Nurture:
  - Day 0: estimate acknowledgment + curated slab links.
  - Day 1: project fit + scheduling options.
  - Day 3: before/after proof + service-area trust.
  - Day 7: deadline-driven follow-up ("hold your install window").

## 4) Social Media Content Plan (Weekly)
- Posting cadence:
  - Instagram: 4 reels + 3 stories/week
  - Facebook: 3 posts/week
  - TikTok: 3 short videos/week
  - Google Business Profile: 2 photo updates + 1 post/week
- Content pillars:
  - Proof: before/after installs, edge details, close-up finish quality.
  - Process: template day, fabrication clips, install day timelapses.
  - Guidance: quartz vs granite vs quartzite, maintenance myths, buyer FAQ.
  - Trust: owner story, team craftsmanship, service-area wins, review screenshots.
- CTA pattern:
  - 70% value-first ("save this for your remodel")
  - 30% direct response ("DM 'QUOTE' or use estimate form")

## 5) 30-Day Content Sprint
- Week 1: 8 homeowner pain-point clips (stains, seams, timeline anxiety).
- Week 2: 6 material-comparison clips + 2 contractor-focused posts.
- Week 3: 6 install proof clips + 2 testimonial highlights.
- Week 4: 4 FAQ clips + 2 offer clips + 2 local service-area reels.

## 6) SEO + Local Authority (Lead Growth Multiplier)
- Continue publishing localized service-area/material pages.
- Add monthly "project spotlight" pages with schema and internal links.
- Request 8-12 new Google reviews/month with photo attachments.
- Build citation consistency across key local directories.

## 7) Paid Amplification (Optional, High ROI)
- Meta lead ads:
  - Retarget site visitors + engagers (30-day window).
  - Offer: "Free curated slab shortlist + estimate in 24h."
- Budget starter: $30-$60/day, optimize for qualified lead forms.

## 8) KPI Dashboard (Track Weekly)
- Traffic: organic sessions, local pack impressions, top landing pages.
- Leads: form submits, chat-qualified leads, sourced leads, call leads.
- Sales: booked appointments, close rate, average ticket, monthly revenue.
- Ops: response time, no-show rate, cycle time from lead to install.

## 9) 90-Day Execution Priorities
1. Tighten lead response SLA and follow-up automation.
2. Publish consistent proof-first social content every week.
3. Scale local SEO pages and review velocity.
4. Expand contractor repeat-program outreach.
5. Review KPI dashboard weekly and reallocate effort to top channels.
